Bachelor Thesis

My bachelor thesis was centered around creating a customizable pipeline for annotating human skeletons in image datasets. This tool is created for researchers and developers working in computer vision, particularly in pose estimation and person/object detection. It integrates seamlessly with MMDetection, MMPose and LabelMe. The final pipeline includes the automated and adjustable process, anonimyzing people in the dataset and different export options, also for manual adjustments.
